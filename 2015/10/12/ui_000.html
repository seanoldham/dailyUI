UI_000


I'm not a designer. I can't draw. I can barely make my clothes match. In an effort to remedy this, I have challenged myself.

Yesterday I found Daily UI.

The premise is simple. Every weekday, over the course of 100 days, I get an email. That email has a prompt. I create a UI element based on that prompt and post it here.

My goal is to complete this challenge and become a better designer in the process. Hopefully this will inspire me to spruce up things around here, too.

Stay tuned and watch me design a new UI element every day for the next 100 days.
